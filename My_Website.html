Why I love movies

I have loved movies ever since I was a little kid. My dad was a Film/TV/Radio college major and he used to make short films before I was born. When I was a little kid, he took me to the movies all the time. He introduced me to his favorites, and helped me develop my own taste in movies. I am grateful that he took the time to share his passion for movies with me, and it is something that we have in common to this day.
